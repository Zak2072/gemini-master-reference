# Social Value Measurement — National TOMs

## Core Directives

- **Reference the Complete Framework**: Ground all analysis, auditing, and advice directly in the 48 explicit National TOMs Core Measures below. Do not approximate or invent KPI identifiers.
- **Data Structuring**: When extracting or evaluating metrics from text, map them exactly to their designated alphanumeric indicator (e.g., NT1, NT14, NT31) and measure type.
- **Granular Unit Enforcement**: Ensure performance tracking matches the strict baseline reporting unit defined by the framework (e.g., FTE, weeks, monetary spend, tonnes, hours, or percentage).

## Workflow

### Step 1: Pillar Assessment

Categorise the incoming target, initiative, or reporting line into its correct National TOMs Theme:

- **Theme 1: Jobs** (NT1 to NT13, NT76)
- **Theme 2: Growth** (NT14 to NT16, NT18 to NT20, NT50, NT74, NT75)
- **Theme 3: Social** (NT17, NT21, NT22, NT24 to NT29, NT63)
- **Theme 4: Environment** (NT31 to NT33, NT35, NT44 to NT46, NT48, NT68, NT70, NT72)
- **Theme 5: Innovation** (NT39 to NT42)

### Step 2: KPI Selection

Cross-reference the initiative's explicit criteria against the 48 Core Measures below to find the specific alphanumeric match.

### Step 3: Validation and Unit Check

Confirm the metric validation paths align with the framework standards (e.g., assessing apprenticeship delivery using "weeks" rather than headcounts).

## National TOMs 48 Core Measures Reference

### Theme 1: Jobs — Promoting Local Skills and Employment

- **NT1**: Number of direct local employees (FTE) hired or retained.
- **NT3**: Number of local people hired who were long-term unemployed (12+ months).
- **NT3d**: Number of local employees hired who are survivors of modern slavery.
- **NT4**: Number of local people hired who were NEETs (Not in Employment, Education, or Training).
- **NT4a**: Number of local 16–25 year old care leavers hired on the contract.
- **NT76**: Number of local people hired who were registered as unemployed.
- **NT5**: Number of rehabilitating or ex-offenders (18+ years) hired on the contract.
- **NT6**: Number of disabled local employees (FTE) hired or retained on the contract.
- **NT8**: Number of career support sessions provided (e.g., CV workshops, mock interviews).
- **NT9**: Number of apprenticeship weeks (Level 2, 3, or 4) delivered on the contract.
- **NT10**: Number of Higher Apprenticeship weeks (Level 4+) delivered on the contract.
- **NT11**: Number of work placement weeks (unpaid/student) delivered on the contract.
- **NT12**: Number of paid internship weeks delivered on the contract.
- **NT13**: Number of training weeks delivered to staff on the contract.

### Theme 2: Growth — Supporting Growth of Responsible Regional Business

- **NT14**: Total spend with local micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs).
- **NT18**: Total spend with local voluntary, community, and social enterprises (VCSEs).
- **NT19**: Total spend with the wider local supply chain.
- **NT20**: Investment in workforce healthy lifestyle schemes (e.g., cycle to work, stop smoking).
- **NT15**: Business mentoring hours provided to local MSMEs or VCSEs.
- **NT16**: Support provided to local suppliers to help them measure and improve their own social value.
- **NT50**: Number of people trained in green skills for a low-carbon transition.
- **NT74**: Presence of union recognition or equivalent worker representation in the supply chain.
- **NT75**: Implementation of Good and Fair Work charters on the contract.

### Theme 3: Social — Healthier, Safer, and More Resilient Communities

- **NT17**: Number of staff volunteering hours donated to local community projects.
- **NT28**: Monetary donations to local community projects or charities.
- **NT29**: In-kind donations of materials or equipment to local community projects.
- **NT21**: Hours dedicated to supporting local crime reduction initiatives.
- **NT24**: Initiatives to help vulnerable people live independently.
- **NT25**: Support for initiatives to tackle homelessness.
- **NT63**: Support for initiatives to help rough sleepers.
- **NT26**: Support for community health or wellbeing interventions.
- **NT27**: Initiatives focused on strengthening community networks and reducing isolation.
- **NT22**: Programmes for community engagement and stakeholder satisfaction.

### Theme 4: Environment — Decarbonising and Safeguarding Our World

- **NT31**: Carbon savings achieved on the contract through energy efficiency measures.
- **NT32**: Savings in car miles driven through transport initiatives.
- **NT33**: Reduction in greenhouse gas emissions (CO₂e) measured in tonnes.
- **NT44**: Implementation of a formal policy and programme to achieve net-zero carbon by 2030.
- **NT45**: Savings in water consumption (M³) against relevant benchmarks.
- **NT46**: Initiatives to improve local air quality (e.g., low emission delivery vehicles).
- **NT35**: Initiatives to protect or enhance biodiversity and the natural environment.
- **NT48**: Percentage of contracts including sustainable procurement commitments.
- **NT68**: Total tonnes of waste diverted from landfill.
- **NT70**: Percentage reduction in single-use plastics on the contract.
- **NT72**: Tonnes of waste reduced through reuse of products or materials.

### Theme 5: Innovation — Promoting Social Innovation

- **NT39 (Jobs)**: New methods to create local skills and employment.
- **NT40 (Business)**: Innovative support for responsible regional business growth.
- **NT41 (Communities)**: Novel ways to enable healthier, safer, and more resilient communities.
- **NT42 (Environment)**: New technologies or practices to respond to the climate emergency.

## Examples

**Good:** "The project will provide 24 weeks of Level 3 apprenticeships on-site, fulfilling metric NT9. This will be tracked and reported in apprenticeship delivery units as required by Theme 1."

**Bad:** "We are supporting employment by hiring 3 local apprentices over the duration of the contract." (Fails to reference the exact NT9 code and uses headcount instead of training weeks.)
